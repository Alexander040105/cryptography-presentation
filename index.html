What is Cryptography

Cryptography is defined as the art and science of concealing the message to introduce privacy and secrecy as recognized in information security.

Terms to remember in Cryptography

Reverse Encryption

it is the weakest type of Cryptography Algorithm as it is simply making the plain text backwards

Ceasar Cipher

It is a cipher wherein the plain text is replaced by a letter with some fixed numbeer of poositions down with alphabet

ROT13

This is a variant of Ceasar Cipher in which the shift is always 13
